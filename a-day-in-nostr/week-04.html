post.20

Nostr moves fast

Mon April 03, 2023

Why does nostr seem to move faster?

From the surface, it may seem impossible to be able to build nostr without a central figure dictating every move. Yet that absence is what makes this protocol move much faster and creatively than its platform counterparts.

Instead of a chaotic mess without purpose or vision, as one might imagine protocols to be, nostr revolves around a number of creative hubs with specific types of development and design. Hubs interact openly with one another, sharing ideas, issues to resolve, and milestones to celebrate... Each hub is a team, and development depends on how well the team can gel while retaining creative independence.

Nostr as a protocol works, in part, because of its guiding principles: Decentralization, simplicity, interoperability...

Another positive aspect is the fun in building something that you interact with everyday. It feels purposeful to dig into issues and to find a way to contribute.

It doesn't mean contributing is perfect, that there isn't overlap or mistakes along the way, but nothing is perfect. Nostr's ability to captivate creative people is what keeps its wheels moving.

post.19

"I got your back." ~nostr

Sun April 02, 2023

What is it like to work on nostr, knowing the protocol is open, accessible, interoperable and is running on every client, relay...?

The nostr protocol is a strong foundation to BUIDL upon, but its use and spread depends on its network effects. The stronger the community bonds, the stronger the protocol. It is a give and take.

Nostr is a market competition, but not to win above all others, instead a competition to find your own unique niche, making sure it is interoperable.

Just like all humans have different strengths and weaknesses, benefitting from working together, nostr products are imperfect by nature and benefit from community. The uniqueness of a client or relay can be its strength, and what could be thought of as a bug or weakness is an area others can fill. Like waves spreading on a sandy beach, leaving a few gaps along the way, other nostr products or tools can fill those existing gaps, creating a flexible mesh that gets stronger over time.

Interoperability means thinking beyond yourself, beyond individual products, into the possibilities to support the whole. #Nostr is an I-got-your-back protocol.

post.18

Nostrica
vibe

Sat Mar 11, 2023

Nostrica may be an in-the-works conference, but it is also a vibe. It carries hope and nostr BUIDLing.

Nostr is not a company, it's a protocol uplifted by a community which often understands decentralization, censorship-resistance and value for value. We journey together through sunrises and sunsets, gm/n's and pura vida's, bugs and features...

The journey to nostrica has been one of bringing together the hearts and minds behind the code. Nostrica will not only be a place to reflect and celebrate on the milestones so far, it will be a place to BUIDL. We can BUIDL on ideas, BUIDL on nostr clients, BUIDL on community. HODL on, nostrica will be a unique time. Grateful to be living it.
